Call for Submissions

Submit Your Story

You are the artist — share your “art” with us.

Do you have a story to tell? We want to read it. A song, a poem, a photograph? We want it. Painting or tattoo, film or fierce honest essay — whatever shape your truth takes, bring it. This magazine belongs to the people living the story.

Pressing the button opens your email app with everything pre-filled, addressed to our editor’s inbox at Submissions@theartofrecoverymag.com. Attach any files (images, audio, PDFs) before you hit send. Readers in recovery and contributors discovered through Issue One — this door is open to you.
